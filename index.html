My Projects

This is my Game on Scratch, called "Shoot the bats".The bats will appear when the flag is clicked. They will glide to a random position, like bats flying around. When you hit the bats, you will get 5 coins. For the grey bat, it will stay still but will fly away when your mouse is touching it, making it difficult to catch. However, if you do catch it, you will get 20 coins instead of the normal 5. You can use your coins to buy various bat catchers in the store. You can also upgrade your game by buying more bats, so you can hit bats more easily and get more coins.

This is my Image Recogniser app, made in Thunkable.The Image Recogniser utilises Microsoft AI technology to inspect pictures taken from the Phone camera, then recognises what item it is in the picture. To use it, take a photo using your phone and wait for the App to tell you the result.

This is my Text Translator app, made in Thunkable. It can translate English into German, Chinese, Malay, and Tamil. The user can enter any English word into the text box, then press the translate buttons to translate into the respective languages. The text to speech will also read it out, so visually impared people can use the app.
